About Me

Behind every edit is a decade-long obsession with light, color and detail.

Adobe CertifiedPhotoshop & Premiere Pro

I'm Ashu — a Mumbai-based photo editor, graphic designer, and content creator with over six years of experience helping brands, photographers, and creators turn good images into unforgettable ones.

My work lives at the intersection of technical precision and cinematic feeling: color grades that evoke a mood, retouching that stays invisible, and designs built to stop the scroll. I've partnered with fashion labels, e-commerce brands, and independent creators across 15+ countries.
